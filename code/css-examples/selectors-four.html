A Persistent World of Rising Thunder

The World of Rising Thunder is a persistent and ongoing world, even across server resets. It is basically, a no majical “operable equipment” spawning concept.

“Operable equipment” is defined as any operable aircraft, vehicles, armor, artillery, naval vessels or troops types, operable by either clients or bots (AI).

The Mission Operations Gadget (MOG) (S3)

The heart of the Rising Thunder experience is the Mission Operations Gadget, and it is the most complicated part of this project! Here is the Description & Operation of the Mission Operations Gadget, starting at the top of the gadget and working down.
