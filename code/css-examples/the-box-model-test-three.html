To arm your aircraft from the Online Operations Board, hold the aircraft you wish to operate. Then right click on your selected aircraft from the aircraft segment of the board. You will be presented with the “DCS Arm and Refuel Dialog Box” for that aircraft, however, depending on your personas rank and weapons availability, your selection of weapon options may be limited.
